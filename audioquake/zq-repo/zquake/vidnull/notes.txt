Applying vidnull to our ZQ repo
Last updated: 02/01/2008

1. Copy the .sln and .vcproj files over from vidnull to zquake/source.
2. Choose the GLRelease config and compile zqds and zquake.
3. Copy the source files over from vidnull to zquake/source.
4. Choose the VNRelease config and compile zquake.
