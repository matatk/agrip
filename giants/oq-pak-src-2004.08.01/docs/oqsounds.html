Open Quartz Needs Sounds

Open Quartz is a project to supply GPL artwork to create a fully GPL game based around the GPL quake sourcecode. We have some new models, maps, and textures, but we still have few sounds. The sounds must be free of copyright restrictions such as royalties which would conflict with the GNU General Public License. This means we can use sounds licensed under the GPL or anything less restrictive.

NOTE: We also need help making two ambient sounds loop: ambience/water1.wav and ambience/wind2.wav need a special "cue list" entry added which will let the engine know when to loop the sound.
